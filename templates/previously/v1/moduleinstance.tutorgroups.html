Labs

Labs are held on a Tuesday afternoon in the main terminal room (A32).

Attendance at labs is compulsory unless you are already submitting perfect or near-perfect solutions to exercises. If you miss a lab session and do not submit that week's exercises without good reason, you will be asked to contact a member of staff regarding the reasons for this, and this may results in your CSIT account being locked.

Groups

You are assigned a group to work in during the labs. This group defines who your demonstrator will be and where abouts you sit. Groups are assigned randomly , and you are not permitted to swap groups or to sit within the designated area of another group during lab times. This allows the demonstrators to learn who their students are more easily and prevents some plagiarism problems.
