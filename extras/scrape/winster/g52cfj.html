Learning Outcomes:  Knowledge and Understanding: Understanding of the theory and practice of procedural (C) and object-oriented (C++) programming. Intellectual Skills: Understand and logically evaluate program requirements and specifictions. Understand the complex ideas of programming solutions and relate them to particular problems. Professional Skills: The ability to write procedural and object-oriented programs in C/C++ . Transferable Skills: The ability to solve problems using programming and communicate the results in writing.
